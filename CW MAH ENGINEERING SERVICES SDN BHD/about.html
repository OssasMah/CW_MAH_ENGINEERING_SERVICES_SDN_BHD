Company Background

CW MAH Engineering Services Sdn Bhd was established in 2016 to meet the requirements of power plant, refinery and chemical plant industries. The company specializes in industrial piping systems and associated engineering works.

Our capabilities cover the full scope of engineering services including material procurement, fabrication, installation, construction, assembly, quality control, pressure testing, testing and commissioning, as well as final handover documentation.

The company is experienced in workshop fabrication, plant construction and on-site commissioning works, delivering projects in accordance with engineering standards and client requirements.
